Our Mission

At GrooveLink, we aim to connect South Africa’s dancers, choreographers, and fans to every major dance event with the click of a button.


We create a vibrant hub to discover competitions, showcase talent, and celebrate our diverse dance scene.


One step ahead, one beat at a time, we bring the community together—on stage, in the audience, or behind the scenes!
