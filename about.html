About GrooveLink

Established in 2025, GrooveLink is South Africa’s go-to platform for discovering, sharing, and celebrating dance culture.


With dance becoming more vibrant in South Africa, many talented South Africans do not know where to find opportunities beyond tiktok and instagram. From major competitions to local workshops, we connect dancers to the rhythm of the nation.


Whether you're a performer, organizer, or fan, GrooveLink makes it easy to stay in the loop. Let’s dance together and keep the culture alive.
