About GrooveLink

Established in 2025, GrooveLink is South Africa's go-to platform for discovering, sharing, and celebrating dance culture.

With dance becoming more vibrant across South Africa, many talented dancers don't know where to find opportunities beyond TikTok and Instagram. From major competitions to local workshops, we connect dancers to the rhythm of the nation.

Whether you're a performer, organizer, or fan, GrooveLink makes it easy to stay in the loop. Let's dance together and keep the culture alive.

What We Stand For

Community

Bringing dancers, schools, and organisers together under one roof.

Culture

Celebrating the rich diversity of South African dance styles.

Opportunity

Making sure no dancer misses out on their next big moment.
